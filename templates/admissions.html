Admission Process

  1. Submit online application form
  2. Initial screening and review
  3. Entrance test (for grades 6 and above)
  4. Parent-student interaction
  5. Admission confirmation

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical records
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Address proof
